Various fans with a 247mm diameter can be used.
The Spal VA07-AP7/C-31A 12V is recommended.
Connection suggestion:
Since the two fan motors draw significantly more current than the stock fan, they should be switched via a load relay.
Permanent positive directly from the battery – via the relay – to the motors. Don't forget the fuse.
Ground to the fans from the central ground point – to the right of the front panel.
The original supply line serves as a switching impulse for the relay.
